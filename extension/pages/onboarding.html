Welcome to ProtoConsent

ProtoConsent puts you in control of how websites use your data. Choose a default privacy profile below. You can always change it or customize individual sites from the toolbar icon.

Choose your operating mode

ProtoConsent can protect you on its own or work alongside an existing ad blocker. You can switch at any time from the toolbar icon or Settings.

Blocking Recommended
Blocks tracking requests directly based on your privacy profile. Full standalone protection.
Monitoring
Works alongside your existing blocker (uBlock Origin, AdGuard, Brave Shields). Adds consent and signals without blocking requests itself.
Both modes include:
✓ GPC privacy signal (legal opt-out)
✓ Cookie banner auto-response
✓ Client Hints fingerprint protection
✓ Site transparency (.well-known)
✓ Per-site control and real-time Log
✓ Enhanced Protection lists

Enhanced lists

ProtoConsent includes bundled enhanced lists. These options let you extend and automate protection. You can change them later in Settings.

Cookie banner auto-response Automatically accepts or rejects cookie banners based on your privacy profile. Covers 50+ consent providers. Active by default.
Cosmetic filtering Hides ad containers and empty banners left after blocking, using EasyList Cosmetic rules. Active by default.
