Cookie Banner Management

Master switch
Cookie banners disappear automatically. ProtoConsent answers them for you, accepting or rejecting each purpose based on your profile.
CMP detection
Detect which consent platforms are present on each page and observe what their cookies say. Results appear in the Overview and Log tabs. Passive - does not modify anything.
CMP list
Advanced
Consent cookies include an identifier. Leave empty (recommended): a unique random ID is generated for each page visit, so sites cannot link your visits. Set a fixed UUID only if a specific site requires it - the same ID will be reused across all visits, allowing sites to recognize you.
Saved
How long injected consent cookies persist. Range: 1-365 days. Default: 90.
